> Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is an Indian international cricketer and one of the most successful batsmen in the world.
> Kohli showed exceptional talent in cricket from a young age and started playing for the Delhi Under-15 team when he was just 14 years old.
> Kohli made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008 in an ODI against Sri Lanka.
> In 2006, he made his debut for the senior Delhi cricket team in domestic cricket.
> In his entire career timeline, he has given many record breaking performances.
